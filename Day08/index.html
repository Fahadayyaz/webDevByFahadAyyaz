Mastering Inline & Block Elements

Tutorial by Fahad

1. Block-Level Elements

Block elements always start on a new line and take up the full width available.

I am a <div>. Notice how I take up the whole width of the page!

I am a <p> (paragraph). I also take the full width.

2. Inline Elements

Inline elements do not start on a new line. They only take up as much width as their content needs.

I am a <span>. I am an <a> (anchor) tag. I am bold.

Notice how the red boxes above stay on the same line? That is the power of inline elements.
